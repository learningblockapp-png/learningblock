How it works

Four simple steps

1

Set it up

Choose your child's year group, select which topics to include, and set how many questions they need to answer.

2

iPad gets locked

Set a daily lock time or lock it manually. When locked, your child sees the Learning Block screen — no way around it.

3

Complete the quiz

Your child works through curriculum-aligned maths questions. Get them all right and the iPad unlocks. No shortcuts.

4

Track progress

See accuracy per topic, daily streaks, stars earned, and 24 trophies to collect. Watch their confidence grow.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Learning Block ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Learning Block app.

Information We Collect

Learning Block is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

What Is Stored Locally

  • Your child's name and chosen emoji avatar
  • Quiz results and topic accuracy
  • Streaks, stars, and trophy progress
  • Parent settings including PIN, year group, and topic selections
  • Lock schedule preferences

This data is stored on-device only and is permanently deleted if you uninstall the app or use the Reset Progress feature in Parent Settings.

Children's Privacy

Learning Block is designed for use by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app does not require an account, does not connect to the internet for any purpose, and does not include advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

Third-Party Services

Learning Block does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline.

In-App Purchases

If you purchase a subscription or lifetime licence, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions and can be found at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device security. We recommend using a device passcode and keeping iOS up to date.

Your Rights

You can delete all data stored by Learning Block at any time by using the Reset Progress option in Parent Settings, or by uninstalling the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@learningblock.app.
