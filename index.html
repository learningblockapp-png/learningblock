Features

Everything you need

📖

Learn it before they're tested on it

Every topic has animated, step-by-step walkthroughs that teach the method before your child faces the quiz. Visual diagrams, worked examples, and clear explanations — all at their own pace. It's not just testing, it's teaching.

✏️

Work it out. For real

Column method, borrowing, and a drawing pad so children can show their working. They learn the method, not just the answer.

⚙️

You set the rules

Schedule, screen time limits, earned sessions, daily caps — all in your hands. Set a bedtime and a wake time. The iPad locks automatically and stays locked until the morning quiz is done.

🔐

They can't get around this one

Uses Apple Screen Time to lock the entire iPad at OS level — not just the app. No workarounds, no way out. When Learning Block says locked, it means locked.

📚

Curriculum-aligned

Questions from the UK National Curriculum for KS1 and KS2, with infinite variety — different every time.

📊

Parent dashboard

Per-topic accuracy, quiz history, and daily streaks. See exactly which areas need attention.

🏆

Collections & badges

24 badges to earn, 56 emoji to collect, and 8 themes to unlock. Progress rewarded.

📱

Lock screen widget

Glance at the home screen to see if it's locked, unlocked, or counting down free time.

🌙

Bedtime schedule

Locks at night, stays locked until the morning quiz. Automatic, every day.

🚫

No ads, ever

No adverts, no distractions, no social features. Just maths, learning, and screen time earned.
