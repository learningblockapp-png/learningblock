How it works

Simple by design

1

Set it up

Choose your child's year group, select topics, and set how many questions they need to answer. Takes two minutes.

2

iPad gets locked

Set a bedtime or lock it manually. When locked, your child sees the Learning Block screen — no way around it.

3

Earn screen time

Complete the quiz, unlock the iPad. Earn XP, collect badges, and unlock themes as progress builds over time.

Features

Everything you need

🔐

OS-level lock

Uses Apple Screen Time to lock the entire iPad at OS level — not just the app. No workarounds, no way out.

📚

Curriculum-aligned

Questions generated from the UK National Curriculum for KS1 and KS2, with infinite variety — different every time.

🕐

Visual questions

Clock faces, shapes, angles, fractions, and number lines — not just text on a screen.

✏️

Show workings

Drawing pad and column maths panel so children can work things out properly, not just guess.

🌙

Bedtime schedule

Set a bedtime and a wake time. The iPad locks automatically at night and stays locked until morning quiz done.

🏆

Collections & unlocks

Level up to unlock themes, celebrations, and emoji avatars. 20 badges to earn by mastering topics.

📊

Parent dashboard

Per-topic accuracy, quiz history, and daily streaks. See exactly which areas your child is strongest in.

📱

Lock screen widget

Glance at the iPad's lock screen to instantly see if it's locked, unlocked, or counting down free time.

🚫

No ads, ever

No adverts, no distractions, no social features. Just maths, learning, and screen time earned.
