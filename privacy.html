1 What we collect

Learning Block stores the following locally on your iPad only. Nothing is sent to us or any third party:

  • Your child's name and chosen emoji avatar
  • Quiz results, streak counts, XP, and badge progress
  • Parent settings — year group, topics, quiz length, PIN, and schedule

✓ Learning Block has no servers. No data ever leaves your device.

All data can be deleted at any time via Reset Progress in Parent Settings, or by deleting the app. If you choose to sign up for email updates on our website, we collect your email address via Mailchimp for occasional product news. This is entirely optional and you can unsubscribe at any time.

2 Payments & third parties

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store — we never see or store your payment details. The app contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. The only external service used is Apple's App Store for subscription management.

To manage or cancel your subscription: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Apple's privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy.

3 Your rights & contact

Learning Block Ltd complies with UK GDPR. Because all data lives on your device, your rights — access, erasure, portability — are exercised directly through the app or by deleting it. We may update this policy from time to time; the date at the top of this page reflects the latest version.
